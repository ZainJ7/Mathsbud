About Us

Welcome to Mathsbud! We are a team of experienced educators and developers who are passionate about helping children aged 7-8 develop their maths skills through engaging and interactive games.

Our goal is to make learning maths fun and accessible for all children, regardless of their background or ability. We believe that every child has the potential to excel in maths, and our games are designed to help them build a solid foundation of key maths concepts, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

At Mathsbud, we believe that learning should be fun and engaging, and we are dedicated to helping children develop a lifelong love of maths. Thank you for choosing Mathsbud, and we hope you and your child enjoy our games!
